Drag & drop your .run files here

or choose below — everything runs locally in your browser

🪟 Can't find the folder? Windows hides it — here's the trick:

%APPDATA%\SlayTheSpire2
  1. Click Select history folder above.
  2. In the dialog's Folder field, paste the path (Ctrl+V) and press Enter.
  3. Click Select Folder — it grabs every run inside automatically.

On macOS the runs live in ~/Library/Application Support/SlayTheSpire2 (press ⌘⇧G in the file dialog and paste it).

🔒 Completely private. Your runs are read and analyzed entirely in your browser — nothing is ever uploaded to a server. Close the tab and it's gone.

Three steps to your dashboard

No install, no account — just your run files.

1

Find your runs

Your runs live in a hidden Windows folder — no need to unhide it. Just copy this path:

%APPDATA%\SlayTheSpire2

2

Paste & select

Click Select history folder, paste the path into the dialog and press Enter, then Select Folder — every run inside is picked up automatically.

3

Explore

Your dashboard builds instantly. Filter by character, ascension and outcome, and dig through every tab. Curious first? Hit Try with sample data.

Three steps to your dashboard

No install, no account — just your run files.

1

Find your runs

Every completed run is a .run file in your save folder:

…\SlayTheSpire2\steam\<id>\profile1\saves\history

2

Drop them in

Use Select history folder and point it at that folder, or drag the .run files straight onto the page.

3

Explore

Your dashboard builds instantly. Filter by character, ascension and outcome, and dig through every tab. Curious first? Hit Try with sample data.
